About SAARATHI

SAARATHI is a non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the educational gap in government schools and fostering financial independence for women.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality educational guidance and resources to children in government schools, ensuring no child is left behind due to financial constraints.

Our Vision

A society where every child has equal access to opportunities and every woman is empowered to live a life of dignity and independence.
