Who We Are

Saarathi is a community-driven non-profit organization dedicated to supporting the education and holistic development of rural students in Karnataka.

Working closely with government schools, we bridge educational gaps by providing essential supplies and digital exposure. We believe every child deserves access to quality learning, regardless of their background.

“We don’t just support education—we build confidence, choices, and hope.”
